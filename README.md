# hr-analytics-dashboard--1
Interactive HR Analytics dashboard analyzing employee attrition, workforce demographics, job satisfaction, and departmental performanc

## Project Overview
This project analyzes employee attrition and workforce demographics using HR data. The dashboard provides insights into employee turnover, job satisfaction, overtime impact, departmental performance, and workforce characteristics to support data-driven HR decision-making.

## Objectives
- Measure employee attrition and retention.
- Identify departments and job roles with high turnover.
- Analyze the impact of overtime on attrition.
- Explore attrition patterns across age groups.
- Monitor workforce demographics and income levels.

## Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)
- Total Employees: 1,470
- Attrition Count: 237
- Attrition Rate: 16.12%
- Average Age
- Average Monthly Income: 6,503

## Dashboard Features
- Interactive filters (Department, Gender, Attrition, Job Role)
- Employee Attrition Trend Analysis
- Attrition Distribution
- Department-wise Attrition Analysis
- Age Group Analysis
- Job Role Analysis
- Overtime Impact Analysis
- Job Satisfaction Analysis
- Education Field Analysis
- Key Business Insights

## Key Insights
- Overall attrition rate is 16.12%.
- Sales department records the highest attrition.
- Sales Representatives experience the highest turnover.
- Employees working overtime are significantly more likely to leave.
- Employees aged 18–24 show the highest attrition rate.
- Research & Development demonstrates stronger employee retention.
